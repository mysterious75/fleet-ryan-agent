## Fleet Knowledge (Baseline)

### What We Know About Fleet Management
- Fleet = group of vehicles owned/operated by a company
- Fleet management = tracking, maintaining, and optimizing those vehicles
- Key data points: location, fuel, maintenance, driver behavior, compliance
- Fleet platforms: Samsara (primary), Motive, Geotab, Fleetio

### Vehicle Lifecycle
- Each vehicle has: ID, make/model/year, VIN, odometer, engine hours
- Vehicles go through: Active → Maintenance → Active (or Retired)
- Maintenance is triggered by: mileage thresholds, fault codes, time intervals
- Common maintenance: oil change (25K mi), tires (25K mi), brakes (50K mi)

### Driver Management
- Each driver has: ID, name, license, HOS (Hours of Service) tracking
- HOS rules: 11hr driving limit, 14hr on-duty window, 30min break after 8hrs
- DVIR = Driver Vehicle Inspection Report (pre/post trip)
- Violations = breaking HOS rules (illegal, can result in fines)

### Fuel Tracking
- Fuel level is a percentage (0-100%)
- Fuel consumption varies by: vehicle type, load, terrain, driving style
- Fuel theft = sudden drop in fuel level without corresponding mileage
- Idling = engine on but vehicle not moving (wastes fuel)

### Fault Codes (DTC = Diagnostic Trouble Codes)
- P-codes = Powertrain (engine, transmission)
- C-codes = Chassis (brakes, suspension)
- B-codes = Body (airbags, lights)
- U-codes = Network/communication
- Severity: Critical (stop immediately) → Warning (schedule service) → Info (monitor)
